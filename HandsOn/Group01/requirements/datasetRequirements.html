Assessment of Selected Datasets vs. Requirements R1–R6

Meets Partially meets / Discuss Does not meet

Requirements Summary

  • R1: Data are in one of the selected smart city domains (Ciudades Abiertas catalog).
  • R2: Data are available as a CSV file.
  • R3: Data have an open license so they can be published.
  • R4: Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities (e.g., locations).
  • R5 (optional): Documentation exists for the data.
  • R6 (optional): Data can come from multiple data sources.

Datasets Evaluated

Dataset 1 — Transportes de la ciudad de Barcelona (Ayuntamiento de Barcelona)

Description: Public transports (metro, Renfe, FGC, funicular, cable car, tramcar, etc.) in Barcelona. Provides CSV and XML distributions; license CC BY 4.0.

R1 (mandatory)
Meets — Falls under Smart City “Transporte” domain.
R2 (mandatory)
Meets — CSV distribution is available for download.
R3 (mandatory)
Meets — Licensed under CC BY 4.0 (open & reusable with attribution).
R4 (mandatory)
Meets — Records include stop/line identifiers and location attributes, enabling linkage to places.
R5 (optional)
Partially meets — Catalog metadata exists, but detailed field-level documentation is limited.
R6 (optional)
Partially meets — Curated by the city portal; mainly a single source though it references multiple operators.

Dataset 2 — Estaciones Rodalies Barcelona (RENFE)

Description: List of Rodalies stations in Barcelona with identifiers, names and geolocation; CSV and XLSX distributions; license CC BY 4.0.

R1 (mandatory)
Meets — Public transport stations are part of the Smart City transportation domain.
R2 (mandatory)
Meets — CSV distribution provided by RENFE portal.
R3 (mandatory)
Meets — Licensed under CC BY 4.0.
R4 (mandatory)
Meets — Includes latitude and longitude fields for easy linkage to locations.
R5 (optional)
Meets — Includes a clear data dictionary describing fields.
R6 (optional)
Partially meets — Data is from RENFE, a single operator, not multiple sources.

Dataset 3 — Funiculares en la ciudad de Barcelona (Ayuntamiento de Barcelona)

Description: Funicular assets for Barcelona; CSV and JSON distributions; license CC BY 4.0.

R1 (mandatory)
Meets — Falls within transport domain.
R2 (mandatory)
Meets — CSV distribution available.
R3 (mandatory)
Meets — Licensed under CC BY 4.0.
R4 (mandatory)
Meets — Includes names and locations, allowing linkage to places.
R5 (optional)
Partially meets — Catalog metadata exists; limited additional documentation.
R6 (optional)
Partially meets — Municipal dataset; no indication of multiple sources.
